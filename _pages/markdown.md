## 🗺️ Global GPS Telemetry Studies Database (MoveDB)

I co-developed an open-access spatial database of published GPS telemetry studies on wild mammals 
across the globe. The database currently holds over 75,000 screened records, each linked to 
species, location, journal, year, and DOI metadata.

**[→ Explore the MoveDB](https://nilanjanchatterjee.github.io/movedb/)**

|---|---|
