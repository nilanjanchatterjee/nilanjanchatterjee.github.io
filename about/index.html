About

I look forward to developing methodological innovations to design studies targeted for different animals. Currently, for my Phd I am trying to estimate population of species captured in motion-triggered camera-traps. I am also an R enthusiast and have been teaching graduate and PhD students about the basics of R programming.

I graduated from the [Indian Statistical Institute](https://www.isical.ac.in/) with a MS in Statistics in 2013. After that, I joined a project on long-term monitoring of carnivores at Widllife Institute of India. Currently, I live in the city of Dehradun at the foothills of the Himalaya. In my free time I like to watch birds, travel to new places(specially in the mountains) and cook.
