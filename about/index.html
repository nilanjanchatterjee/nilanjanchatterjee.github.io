About

I like to analyze data to answer research questions and test hypotheses. Currently, for my Phd I am trying to estimate population of species captured in motion-sensored camera-traps. I am also a R enthusiast and taught graduate and PhD students about the basics of R programming. I graduated from the [Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata](http://www.isical.ac.in/) with a MS in Statistics in 2013. After that, I joined a long-term monitoring project at Widllife Institute of India. Currently, I live in the city of Dehradun at the foothills of Himalaya. In my free time I like to watch birds, travel to new places(specially in the mountains), cook.
